# Advertise & sponsorship setup

The site has an **Advertise** page at `/advertise` for paid sponsorship and ad inquiries. The route is registered in `server.js`, and an “Advertise” link appears in the footer across all main pages.

Advertising and sponsorship inquiries are directed to **admin@mile12warrior.com**, which you already use, so no extra mailbox setup is required.

## Page contents (for your reference)

The Advertise page includes: audience summary, what you offer (banners, sponsored content, newsletter, custom partnerships), brand-fit guidelines (strong fit vs. what you don’t accept), FTC-style disclosure wording, and a clear “Get in touch” call to action with **admin@mile12warrior.com**. Terms of Service (Section 8) state that paid/sponsored content will be clearly labeled in line with FTC guidelines.
