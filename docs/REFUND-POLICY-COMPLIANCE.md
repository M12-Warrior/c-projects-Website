# Refund Policy — Compliance Note (Internal)

**For Mile 12 Warrior LLC internal reference.** This summarizes how our refund and return policy is structured to comply with federal and California law. The actual policy is in the **Terms of Service** (Section 7) at `/terms#refunds` and is linked as "Refund Policy" across the site.

---

## Legal Basis

- **California Civil Code § 1723:** Requires retailers that limit refunds/returns to **conspicuously display** their policy (e.g., at register, entrance, on order forms, or online before purchase). The policy must state: type of refund (cash/credit/exchange), time period, merchandise covered, and any other conditions. If not displayed, consumers may be entitled to a full refund within 30 days. We display our policy in the Terms, at checkout, and via links from the shop and footer.

- **FTC Mail, Internet, or Telephone Order Merchandise Rule (16 CFR Part 435):** Requires timely shipment or, if we cannot ship within the promised time (or 30 days if no time stated), we must notify the customer and refund without being asked if they do not consent to a delay. Refunds should be processed within seven business days for card payments where required.

- **California AB 2426 (effective Jan 1, 2025):** For digital goods, requires clear disclosure that the sale is a **license** (not ownership) and that access can be subject to restrictions or revocation in certain circumstances. Our Terms Section 7 states that digital products (course, packets) are licensed and describes the no-refund-after-access rule and limited 7-day exception.
