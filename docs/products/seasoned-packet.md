# Product sheet: Seasoned Driver Packet

**Internal slug:** `seasoned-packet`  
**Type:** Digital — downloadable / printable packet

---

## Summary

An **advanced** safety and wellness packet for drivers with meaningful road experience (positioning is typically **two or more years** — align with live site copy). Topics skew toward refresher knowledge, long-term health, self-audit habits, and mentorship-oriented thinking.

---

## Who it’s for

- Experienced drivers who want a structured refresher
- Peer trainers or mentors looking for a shared vocabulary with newer drivers
- Safety-minded individuals focused on career longevity, not just “passing the test”

---

## What’s included (high level)

- Multiple sections covering HOS refresher concepts, fatigue science at a deeper level, seasonal hazards, regulatory self-check ideas, and related themes per current product description
- Print-ready format for cab or terminal use

---

## Staff talking points

- “This is the **Tier 2** mental model — not beginner basics only.”
- “Good for someone who already knows the job but wants to **reset** habits.”
- Fleet buyers may combine with **Fleet Refresher Packet** for formal programs — clarify fleet vs individual licensing in **Terms**.

---

## Pairs well with

- **90-Day Course** (for fleets mixing new and experienced hires)
- **Complete Bundle**

---

## Compliance & accuracy

Regulations change; packet content is educational. Encourage verification against current FMCSA and state resources.
