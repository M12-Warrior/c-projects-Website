# Product sheet: New Driver Packet

**Internal slug:** `new-driver-packet`  
**Type:** Digital — downloadable / printable packet

---

## Summary

A foundational safety and wellness packet aimed at **new** CDL drivers. It is organized into multiple print-ready sections covering HOS basics, fatigue, inspections, emergency protocols, and daily routines. Useful as a **desk or cab reference**, not as standalone legal advice.

---

## Who it’s for

- New drivers who learn well from paper or PDF checklists
- Training coordinators who want a consistent handout alongside orientation
- Individuals preparing for the first months on the road

---

## What’s included (high level)

- Numerous sections (specific count in live product description) with practical checklists and educational framing
- References to **DVIR-style** and **HOS** concepts as study material — verify current rules at FMCSA
