# Product sheet: 90-Day Onboarding Course

**Internal slug:** `course-90day`  
**Type:** Digital — structured online course with certificate

---

## Summary

A self-paced onboarding program for new commercial drivers, organized around the first months in the role. It combines HOS awareness, fatigue management, inspections, emergency preparedness, defensive driving, and practical routines. Position it as **skills and habits for the job**, not a substitute for employer training or FMCSA compliance programs.

---

## Who it’s for

- New CDL holders entering their first driving job
- Carriers onboarding new hires who need a consistent baseline
- Individuals who want a guided path before or during early employment

---

## What’s included (high level)

- Multiple modules and lessons with knowledge checks (exact counts may be updated in product copy)
- Certificate of completion when requirements are met
- Aligned with educational framing around **FMCSA Part 395 (HOS)** and related topics as **learning context** — not legal certification
