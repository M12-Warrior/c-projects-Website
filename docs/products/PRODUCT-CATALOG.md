# Mile 12 Warrior — Internal product catalog (training)

**Purpose:** Uniform onboarding for staff, contractors, and promotion partners. Use this to teach *what we offer*, *who it is for*, and *how to describe it accurately*.

**Prices:** Intentionally omitted. Pricing changes; refer to **Admin → Shop** or the live site when money is discussed.

**Accuracy:** Product details should match customer-facing copy and the `products` table in production. When in doubt, verify in Admin or ask the product owner before external use.

---

## Educational disclaimer (read in onboarding)

Mile 12 Warrior provides **educational information**, not legal, medical, or regulatory advice. Training materials and digital packets reference FMCSA and related frameworks as **learning aids**. Drivers and carriers must **verify current regulations** at [fmcsa.dot.gov](https://www.fmcsa.dot.gov) and [dot.ca.gov](https://dot.ca.gov) (and applicable state law). Staff should **not** promise specific regulatory outcomes or present site content as a substitute for qualified safety or legal counsel.

## Classroom session outline (suggested 45–60 min)

1. **Intro (5 min)** — Brand mission; who Mile 12 Warrior serves (professional drivers, fleets). Read disclaimer above.
2. **Digital ladder (15 min)** — New Driver Packet → Course → Seasoned → Fleet packets → Bundles. Walk through [course-90day.md](course-90day.md), [new-driver-packet.md](new-driver-packet.md), [seasoned-packet.md](seasoned-packet.md), then fleet sheets.
3. **Subscriptions (5 min)** — [trucker-wellness-journal-monthly.md](trucker-wellness-journal-monthly.md) vs physical [trucker-wellness-journal.md](trucker-wellness-journal.md).
4. **Physical line (10 min)** — Vest (visibility/safety culture), shirt (brand), kit (gift/onboarding).
5. **Q&A (10 min)** — Refunds and policies: point to **Terms** and **Refund Policy** on the site; do not invent rules.
6. **Takeaway** — Each learner knows where the **single source sheet** lives for their product.
