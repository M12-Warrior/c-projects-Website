# Product sheet: Fleet Seasoned Driver Refresher Packet

**Internal slug:** `fleet-refresher-packet`  
**Type:** Digital — fleet-licensed printable materials

---

## Summary

A **fleet-facing** refresher packet for drivers who are past initial onboarding — typically positioned for **annual or semi-annual** safety meetings. Includes fatigue self-assessment concepts, seasonal hazard calendars, regulatory self-audit themes, and sign-off documentation aligned with ongoing safety culture (references to **49 CFR 391.51** themes in training materials as described on site).

---

## Who it’s for

- Fleet safety teams running recurring refreshers
- Operations leaders who want one consistent refresher deck across terminals
- Carriers pairing new-hire and experienced-driver programming
