# Product sheet: Fleet New Hire Orientation Packet

**Internal slug:** `fleet-new-hire-packet`  
**Type:** Digital — fleet-licensed printable materials

---

## Summary

A **fleet-facing** new-hire orientation packet for safety and HR teams. Designed for structured onboarding: policies, procedures, regulatory touchpoints as **education**, and sign-off documentation concepts aligned with motor carrier DQ file practices (e.g. discussion of **49 CFR Part 391** driver qualification themes in training — not a filing service).

---

## Who it’s for

- Safety directors and training managers at motor carriers
- HR staff supporting CDL driver onboarding
- Consultants helping small fleets stand up orientation

---

## What’s included (high level)

- Multiple sections: FMCSA-oriented onboarding topics, drug/alcohol testing awareness, accident reporting concepts, policy templates as **starting points**, and formal driver sign-off sheets per product positioning
- **Per-company** printing and distribution model — unlimited use **within the purchasing organization** as defined in Terms

---

## Staff talking points

- “Sold to the **company**, not per-seat in the consumer sense.”
- “Helps fleets **standardize** the first-week story.”
- Always distinguish **training materials** from **legal review** — carriers should have counsel approve policy language.

---

## Pairs well with

- **Fleet Refresher Packet** — for the annual cycle
- **Fleet Bundle** — both fleet packets together

---

## Compliance & accuracy

Do not claim the packet alone satisfies all FMCSA training or DQ file requirements. Carriers must maintain compliance with current regulations and company-specific obligations.
