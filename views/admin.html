Fleets & yard licenses

Each fleet packet is licensed for one yard / terminal for 1 year. A fleet with multiple yards needs one packet per yard. Use this panel to view licenses, fix a yard identifier, renew (+1 year), revoke, or manually add a yard license.

Tax & compliance reference

  • Federal LLC: pass-through reporting, self-employment tax, quarterly estimated payments.
  • California LLC: $800 annual franchise tax plus income-based LLC fee thresholds.
  • California digital goods are generally non-taxable when sold standalone; verify for bundled offers.
  • Sacramento and Riverside business licensing rules can apply based on where operations occur.

Verify current requirements at fmcsa.dot.gov, ftb.ca.gov, and cdtfa.ca.gov. For legal/tax interpretation, consult a CPA or attorney.
